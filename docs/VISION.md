# JARVIS Vision

## Five-Year Direction

Every computer should have an AI that understands what you're trying to accomplish, chooses the right tools, works safely, learns continuously, and remains under your control.

JARVIS exists to become that system.

---

## Principles That Don't Change

- **Local-first** — core functionality works offline, on your hardware
- **Capability over provider** — users request outcomes, not tools
- **Learning is default** — every execution improves future results
- **User controls the system** — not the other way around
- **Explainability** — every decision is visible and auditable

---

## Where We're Going

### v3.x — The AI Workspace (current)
A local-first workspace that takes goals, plans them, routes to the best provider, executes, and learns. Six interfaces: Home, Chat, Tasks, History, System, Settings.

### v4 — The Platform
Plugin marketplace for community providers. Mobile companion. Self-modification experiments. Artifact Manager for cross-session state.

### v5+ — Distributed & Autonomous
Multi-machine execution. Collaborative AI agents. Continuous autonomous improvement under user-defined guardrails.

---

## What Success Looks Like

> Someone installs JARVIS, sees a demo in 20 seconds, understands the pipeline in 60 seconds, and finishes thinking: *"I understand how this system works."*

The product is successful when users don't think about subsystems. They think about goals.
